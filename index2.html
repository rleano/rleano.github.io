About me

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at Oregon State University (OSU), where I study/work as a Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Anita Sarma.

Currently, my focus is on collaborative software development. I address issues such as task assignment and conflict resolution. My aim is to build tools that aid developers to work better together. For more details please refer to the appropriate pages below.
