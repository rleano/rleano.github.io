Research

My main research interest is collaborative software development. Specifically, I aim to aid developers with their tasks and the common issues they deal with when working with others (specially in distributed collaboration).

One example is to help developers deal with software conflicts by enhancing workspace awareness. Providing additional information helps both prevent information overload and assist developers prioritize their conflicts.

Additionally, enhancing the task context (i.e. the information we have of a task) can be used to solve task assignment in both directions: (1) Suggesting capable developers for a specific task (task → developers), and (2) recommending tasks that a developer can work on (developer → tasks)
